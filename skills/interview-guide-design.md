## Interview Guide Design

This skill turns an approved research plan into a moderator-ready interview guide, ensuring every research objective is covered while leaving room for natural, unscripted conversation.

### Core Capability

Given the research plan — its objectives, participant criteria, and methodology — this skill produces a semi-structured interview guide: pre-interview setup, opening questions, objective-mapped core questions with probing follow-ups, closing questions, and moderator notes with timing guidance.

### Method

1. **Objective mapping:** Read the research plan and derive 2-3 open-ended core questions per objective, sequenced broad to specific and free of leading language.
2. **Probe preparation:** For each core question, prepare clarification, example, contrast, and impact probes for predictable surface-level answers.
3. **Flow and timing:** Frame the guide as a conversation — rapport-building opening, substantive core, reflective close — and allocate time to fit the target interview duration.

### Output Structure

A printable interview guide with clear section headings, numbered questions, indented probes, and marginal timing notes. It derives from the research plan and stands alongside the survey as a research instrument.
